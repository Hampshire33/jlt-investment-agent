How This Prototype Reaches the Verdict

  • Step 1: Calculate gross yield from annual rent divided by asking price.
  • Step 2: Deduct 5% vacancy and tower service charge to calculate net rent.
  • Step 3: Calculate net yield using net rent divided by asking price.
  • Step 4: Estimate 5-year IRR using entry cost, exit cost, rent growth, price growth and sale proceeds.
  • Step 5: Compare IRR against 3.5% bank benchmark.
  • Step 6: Calculate 6.5% strong-buy price and fair price zone.
  • Step 7: Apply quality score as an overlay, but do not allow quality narrative to override weak economics.
  • Step 8: Output verdict: STRONG BUY, BUY, SELECTIVE BUY, WATCH or PASS.

Master rule: Alpha = entry price. The agent must not recommend buying based only on timing, hype, tower reputation, or gross yield.
