Readiness-status logic:
- DECISION_READY: high-confidence direct/strict evidence.
- INTERIM_SCREENED: Property Finder DLD-derived sale/rent evidence plus DLD service charge input, usable for screening.
- NEEDS_EVIDENCE: missing sale/rent or insufficient usable evidence.
- BUDGET_MONITOR: normal median price above active/default budget range, but keep price/rent values.
- DEAL_TEST: scenario only.
- REVIEW_REQUIRED: should be exceptional, not used for investable rows.

Calculation rules:
- Net rent = annual rent − area × service charge.
- Net yield = net rent / asking price × 100.
- Base strong-buy net-yield trigger = 6.50%.
- Risk-adjusted target yield = 6.75% if FS < 0 or Proxy LEI < 0.
- Target price = net rent / risk-adjusted target yield.
- Required cut = asking price − target price.
- Required cut % = required cut / asking price × 100.
- Purchase-cost assumption = 6.6%.
- Bank benchmark = 3.50% unless updated by bank branch.
- IRR spread = property IRR − bank benchmark.

Macro branch:
- Use S&P Global PMI, Reuters where available, DFM, Central Bank UAE, HSBC/bank evidence.
- FS score:
  +1 supportive/stable
  0 mixed/neutral/incomplete
  -1 stress/caution
- Proxy LEI:
  +1 improving
  0 mixed/neutral
  -1 weakening/uncertain
- Macro must remain overlay only. It tightens target yield but does not override property economics.

Bank branch:

- Source quality: HIGH.
